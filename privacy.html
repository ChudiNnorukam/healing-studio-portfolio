Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 19, 2025
Effective Date: January 19, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Chudi's Trauma Healing Content Studio ("we," "our," or "us"). This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with our content automation tools and trauma-informed professional development resources.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices, especially given the sensitive nature of trauma healing content.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, professional title when you reach out to us
  • Professional Information: Industry, role, experience level for content customization
  • Content Preferences: Topics of interest, content format preferences
  • Feedback: Comments, suggestions, and testimonials you share

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: How you interact with our content and tools
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information
  • Analytics: Page views, time spent, content engagement metrics

2.3 Social Media Integration

  • Platform Data: Information from Pinterest, Instagram, LinkedIn when you interact with our content
  • Public Posts: Content you engage with or share publicly
  • Profile Information: Basic public profile data from connected platforms

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Content Creation and Automation

  • Generate personalized trauma healing and professional development content
  • Automate content posting across social media platforms
  • Customize content recommendations based on your professional needs
  • Improve content quality and relevance

3.2 Communication and Support

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Send relevant updates about new content and features
  • Share trauma-informed professional development resources
  • Provide consultation services when requested

3.3 Analytics and Improvement

  • Analyze content performance and engagement
  • Improve our automation tools and processes
  • Understand user needs and preferences
  • Optimize content delivery and timing

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4.1 Limited Sharing

We may share information only in these specific circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who help us operate our tools (hosting, analytics, automation platforms)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • Business Transfer: In the unlikely event of a business sale or merger
  • Consent: When you explicitly authorize sharing

4.2 Social Media Platforms

When you connect social media accounts for content automation:

  • We only access permissions you explicitly grant
  • Content is posted according to your preferences and schedule
  • We follow each platform's privacy and content guidelines
  • You can revoke access at any time through platform settings

5. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data transmission uses SSL/TLS encryption
  • Access Controls: Limited access on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular Updates: Security systems are regularly updated and monitored
  • Data Minimization: We collect only necessary information
  • Secure Storage: Information is stored on secure, encrypted servers

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to certain uses of your information

6.2 Social Media Controls

  • Disconnect social media accounts at any time
  • Modify content automation settings
  • Control what content is shared and when
  • Review and approve content before posting

6.3 Communication Preferences

  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Choose frequency of updates
  • Select content topics of interest

7. Sensitive Information and Trauma Content

Special Protections for Trauma-Related Content

Given the sensitive nature of trauma healing content, we take extra precautions:

  • Confidentiality: Personal trauma experiences shared with us are kept strictly confidential
  • Anonymization: Any shared stories or examples are thoroughly anonymized
  • Consent: Explicit consent is required before using any personal healing experiences
  • Professional Standards: We follow trauma-informed care principles in all content
  • Safe Practices: Content is reviewed for potential triggers and safety

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze website performance and usage
  • Provide personalized content recommendations
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

You can control cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect functionality.

Our services integrate with:

  • Pinterest API: For content distribution and engagement tracking
  • Instagram API: For automated content posting and community building
  • LinkedIn API: For professional content sharing and networking
  • Analytics Services: For understanding content performance
  • Hosting Services: For website and data hosting

These services have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure adequate protection through appropriate safeguards and compliance with applicable data protection laws.

11. Children's Privacy

Our services are designed for adults (18+) working in professional environments. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the information immediately.

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes via email or through our website. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
